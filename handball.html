Rules

1.There are seven players on each team (six court players and one goalie).
2.A maximum of 12 players may dress and participate in a game for each team.
3.Substitutes may enter the game at any time through own substitution area as long as the player they are replacing has left the court.


4. Uniform shirts and shorts are the same color. The goalkeeper must wear a different color shirt from teammates and opponents.
5. Referees have complete authority: Their decisions are final. The referees are assisted by a timer and a scorer.


6.A player is allowed to use the torso of the body to obstruct an opponent with or without the ball. However, using the outstretched arms or legs to obstruct, push, hold, trip or hit is NOT allowed.
7.A player is allowed to use the torso of the body to obstruct an opponent with or without the ball. However, using the outstretched arms or legs to obstruct, push, hold, trip or hit is NOT allowed.
